Alert Workflow Example

How planners convert AI alerts into concrete actions.
  1. Review AI Explanation

    Planner inspects the main drivers (stock age, velocity, promotions, views) and confirms that the alert makes sense.

  2. Choose an Action Template

    E.g., “Bundle & markdown”, “Transfer to outlet”, “Digital promo boost”, or “Do nothing for now”.

  3. Assign Owner & Due Date

    The system assigns the alert to a specific planner or category manager and tracks completion status.

  4. Track Impact

    After the action, the AI updates risk scores and calculates recovered value, feeding into the Reports page.
